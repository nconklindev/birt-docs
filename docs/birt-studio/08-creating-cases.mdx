## Introduction

This article will review the recommended information to provide to Support when creating a Support case regarding BIRT
Studio or troubleshooting a report. The information will be provided in a template style that will be easy to copy from
this site or the page source and into a new case. Tabs are provided to switch between the provided templates for your
specific situation and can be linked to share with others.

:::tip

When copying the information from the provided template, Support only needs your responses to the bolded item. Please
delete the provided description of the item when submitting a case. If something is not applicable to your case, please
put N/A.

:::
